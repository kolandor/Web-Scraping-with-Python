Initial:

pip install scrapy
scrapy startproject <name of project>
scrapy genspider <spider name> <site>
scrapy crawl <spider name>

Save result:

scrapy crawl <spider name> -o file.csv
scrapy crawl <spider name> -o file.json

EXAMPLE:
scrapy startproject quot
scrapy genspider spid_qou https://quotes.toscrape.com/
scrapy crawl spid_qou -o quotes_result.json
